   >Problem statement:
1. Add exercise with duration and calorie burn rate.
2. Add food items with calorie values.
3. Calculate daily total calories burned and consumed.
4. Compare both to show whether the user is in calorie surplus,   
      deficit ,or balanced.
5. Display a summary report in a readable format.

   >The project scope includes:
1. Allow user to log exercises along with duration and calories                                       
     Burned.
2. Recording food items with calorie values consumed throughout 
      the day.
3. Automatically calculating :
     ~Total calories burned
      ~Total calories consumed
       ~Net calorie difference
4. Displaying daily summaries and progress report in a user-
      format.
5. Providing basic feedback such as, whether the user is in calorie 
     deficit , surplus or balance.

  >Target User:
The fitness and calorie Tracker is designed  for individuals who want to monitor and improve their health habits. The target users include:
1.	Fitness Enthusiasts:
People who regularly exercise and want to track workouts , calories burned ,and performance progress.
2.	Individuals Looking to Lose or Gain Weight:
    User who needs to maintain a calorie deficit or surplus for achieving
    wealth related goals.     
3.	Health-Conscious Individuals:
People who want to adopt a heathier lifestyle by monitoring diet and daily calorie intake.
4.	Beginners Starting a Fitness Journey:
Users who need a simple tool to understand their eating patterns and physical activity levels.
5.	Diet Monitors or Patients with Medical  Guidelines:
     Individual who need to track calories as advised by nutritionists or    
     doctors.
6.	Students Learning or Demonstrating Fitness-Related Software Concepts:
Learners  who use the application to understand 
            programming logic and health- tracking systems.
